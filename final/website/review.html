Shin Route Review

Shin's Route begins with the heroine awaking in a hospital for a fall over a cliff she had awhile before she became amnesiac. Shin, her boyfriend, is accused of shoving the heroine. He says he didn't do it, but did he? The route's focus is on the heroine snooping about to solve the mystery of the events of her fall. Did he do it? Was it an accident? Will he try again?

It's rife with mysteries to keep you entertained! The romance between Shin and the heroine is rather cute too. Shin's personality feels a little rough at first- mostly because he doesn't know when to keep his mouth shut. He's honest, brutally so, and to the point it's a fault. He's rather critical as well, but Shin's harder on himself than anyone.

Shin's idea of romance is that of a five year old on a playground kicking sand in the face of the girl he likes. He's younger than the heroine and which gives him great insecurity, so he doubles his efforts to be in charge and responsible- which can sometimes come off as overbearing, and other times it's rather hilarious to watch him try so hard.

Playing his route, it is rather easy to guess which option will affect which parameters, and his endings aren't difficult to unlock. Shin's route is a great beginner's route!
