Shin In-Depth Guide

Welcome to Shin's In-Depth Walkthrough! All choices will be presented for every date of the game alongside the parameters that are affected when a choice is made! The essential "guide to find all the endings, or if you want to experiment with parameters on your own!

Options in gray do not affect your parameters. You can safely choose to choice to not affect any of your parameters if the other option is something negative (or positive, if you're trying to get a bad ending.) Options in dark red are negative, they either raise your suspicion or lower your trust or affection. Options that are in white raise your trust. Options in pink raise your affection or unlock a image for your image gallery.

For the prologue, none of your answers except for the last, matter. It affects no parameters, and is only essentially in giving you information on the story and how to play. The last question asked presents you with "which world would you like to go to?" To play Shin's route, you have to choose Heart World!

    21. If you do not have enough trust or affection, you will receive the BAD ENDING: "May you dream forever."

      If you have about 50% trust, you will receive the NORMAL ENDING: "I will steal you away."

      If you have max trust, or max affection, you will receive the GOOD ENDING: "I love you. I really love you." There will be various options for the good ending to read through, though they make no changes to your ending no matter what you choose. They're just fun! I suggest saving at this point and exploring through all the options!
