Chunking is Good

This is a good example of web design that does takes proper care of chunking, in which information is separated. Information should be broken down into sections, and the eye should travel a page quickly to find what they need. Use of headers, and spacing will help. So, chunk your information, then chunk it more. That's the key to organization.

Coding

Speaking of, we chunk while coding as well, don't we? We probably could code the entirety of a webpage in one long hideous line. That would be very difficult to debug or change though, and even worse for someone else who never wrote the code in the first place!
